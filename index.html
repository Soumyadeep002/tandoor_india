India is famous for its exotic cuisine prepared with artful and exquisite combination of spices. The igloo-shaped clay oven known as the tandoor is most closely identified with Indian cooking. This is an excellent method of baking meats, poultry, fish and bread. This method of cooking originated in Afghanistan and was brought to India by the Mughal rulers.
